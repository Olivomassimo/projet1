The most popular visit periods are mid-December-August, late December, the spring break of US universities (March / April) and the Japanese Golden Week (late April-early May). The temperatures are warm all year round, with 2 main seasons: a dry season (Apr.-Oct.) And a rainy season (Nov.-Mar.), Better for surfing and whale watching. The main events include the Aloha Festivals (all islands, Sept.), the opportunity to celebrate Hawaiian culture, the Ironman Triathlon World Championships (Big Island, Oct.) and the Honolulu Marathon (Dec.). one of the most important in the world.
